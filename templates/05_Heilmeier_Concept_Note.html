Heilmeier Concept Note

A structured 3-page funding pitch that synthesises all four modular artefacts into the seven Heilmeier questions.
Due Weeks 11–12

The Heilmeier Concept Note is the final synthesis deliverable — a structured 3-page maximum funding pitch that draws directly from your four modular artefacts. Write it in weeks 11–12 after you have completed the Theory of Change, Bottleneck Statement, Field Mapping Synthesis, and Transition Landscape Memo.

Structure and page budget

SectionContentSource artefact
What are you trying to do?One-sentence goal, no jargon. The sharpest version of your elevator pitch.Theory of Change §1–2
How is it done today?Current practice and its specific limits. The bottleneck stated crisply.Bottleneck Statement §1–2
What is new in your approach?Why your intervention is the right lever. What changes if you fund this.Bottleneck §3–4 + Field Mapping §4
Who cares?Named transition actors and what they get. The downstream unlock.Transition Memo §1–2
What are the risks?Technical, structural, and transition risks with mitigations.Theory of Change §5 + Transition §3
How much will it cost, and how long?ROM budget and timeline. Sketch of phases.Program design (2-pager)
Why now / why you?The counterfactual: why this won’t happen without your program.Theory of Change §2 + Field Mapping §6
